Networking

Computer networking refers to interconnected computing devices that can exchange data and share resources with each other. These networked devices use a system of rules, called communications protocols, to transmit information.

Cyber security

Cyber security refers to every aspect of protecting an organization and its employees and assets against cyber threats. As cyberattacks become more common, corporate networks grow more complex.

Software Dev

Software development refers to a set of computer science activities dedicated to the process of designing, and supporting software. Software itself is the set of instructions that tell a computer what to do.

Folio is a website that promotes PTEC study and achievements. It explains the PTEC portfolio, why its was formed, the courses offered, and how the program works. PTEC prepares students for careers in software development, cybersecurity, and networking.
How does PTEC work?
In grades 9 & 10, students take their compulsory courses and their technical courses at Pembina Trails Collegiate. After learning and studying the knowledge-based IT curriculum in both Software development and Cyber Security, students move to the MITT in one of two streams while they continue taking their compulsory courses at Pembina Trails Collegiate.

• One year certificate: Cyber Security Analyst

• One year certificate: Software Developer Stream
